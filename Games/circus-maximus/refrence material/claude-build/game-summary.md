# Roman Circus Game Summary

## Overview
A competitive strategy game for 2-4 players set in ancient Rome. Players compete to put on the best gladiator shows and arena entertainment. **First player to reach 10 on any popularity track wins.**

## Victory Condition
Reach **10 points** on any one of three tracks:
- **Citizens** - Popularity with common people
- **Empire** - Favor with the Emperor/military
- **Clergy** - Approval from religious leaders

---

## Round Structure

Each round has 5 phases:

### Phase 1: Bidding
- 3 acts must be selected (Act 1, Act 2, Act 3)
- Each act has 3 possible show cards to choose from
- Players spend coins (1 at a time) to bid on which card gets picked
- Highest bidder "wins" that act and gets bonus rewards when it's performed

### Phase 2: Worker Placement
- Players take turns placing workers at locations around Rome
- Each placement costs 1-2 coins
- Most locations can only be visited once per round (except Prison)

### Phase 3: Markets
- Players who queued at market locations can now buy resources
- Resources are priced 1-5 coins, cheapest sells first
- Three markets: Slaves, Animals, Mummers

### Phase 4: The Show
- All three acts are performed in order
- Players participate if they have required resources
- Winners gain track points and coins
- Non-participants lose track points

### Phase 5: Cleanup
- Pay feeding costs (1 coin per resource owned)
- Collect income based on track positions
- Restock markets
- Check for winner

---

## Resources

| Resource | Use |
|----------|-----|
| **Slaves** | Gladiator fights, battles, Guildhall |
| **Animals** | Animal shows, chariot races, beast executions |
| **Mummers** | Musicians, chariot races |
| **Prisoners** | Executed in Act 3 for points |
| **Coins** | Currency for everything |
| **Workers** | Placed at locations for actions |

---

## Worker Locations

### Resource Gathering (Risky)
- **Port** - 50% chance: +2 mummers OR worker dies
- **War** - 50% chance: +2 slaves OR worker dies
- **Forest** - 50% chance: +2 animals OR worker dies

### Safe Actions
- **Prison** - +1 prisoner (can visit multiple times)
- **Town Square** - +1 Citizen track
- **Palace** - +1 Empire track
- **Pantheon** - +1 Clergy track

### Special
- **Guildhall** - Pay 5 coins + 1 slave → gain new worker
- **Oracle** - Pay 1 animal → peek at event card
- **Gamblers Den** - Bet on Act 2 outcome

### Markets (Queue for buying)
- **Slave Market** - Queue to buy slaves
- **Animal Market** - Queue to buy animals
- **Mummer Market** - Queue to buy mummers

## Act Cards

### Act 1 (Entertainment)
| Card | Cost | Victory | No Entry Penalty |
|------|------|---------|------------------|
| Musicians | 2 Mummers | All: +1 Clergy, +2 coins | -1 Clergy |
| Animal Show | 1+ Animals | Most animals: +1 Clergy, +1 Citizen, +4c | -1 Clergy, -1 Citizen |
| Man vs Beast | 1 Slave + 1 Animal | All: +2 Citizen, +3 coins | -2 Citizen |

### Act 2 (Competition - dice-based)
| Card | Cost | Victory | Defeat | No Entry |
|------|------|---------|--------|----------|
| Chariot Race | 1 Animal + 1 Mummer | 1st: +2 Empire, +6c / 2nd: +1 Empire, +3c | Resources lost, +2c | -1 Empire |
| Gladiator Duel | 1 Slave | Winner: +2 Citizen, +5c | Slave lost, +3c | -1 Citizen |
| Grand Battle | 2 Slaves | Winner: +2 Empire, +7c | Slaves lost, +4c | -2 Empire |

### Act 3 (Executions)
| Card | Cost | Effect |
|------|------|--------|
| Public Torture | X Prisoners | +1 Citizen per prisoner |
| Crucifixion | X Prisoners | +1 Clergy per prisoner |
| Execution by Beast | X Prisoners + 1 Animal | +1 Empire per prisoner |

---

## Event Cards
Random events that affect each round:
- Caesar Leaves for War - Empire track frozen
- Rome is Raided - Remove 1 resource from each market
- Pirates Grow Brave - Everyone pays 5 coins
- New Lands Discovered - Add 3 animals to supply
- The Plague Strikes - Citizen track frozen

---

## Game Setup
- 2 players: 7 workers each
- 3 players: 6 workers each
- 4 players: 5 workers each
- Starting coins: 50
- Starting track positions: 2 on each track
